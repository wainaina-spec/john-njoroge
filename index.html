Find Answers to All Your Questions Here

You've more questions? Send them to: johnwainaina385@gmail.com

  • How much will it cost to build my website?

    The cost of designing your website depends on your website needs. I use a value-based pricing model. Therefore, I’m only able to determine the exact cost after we have discussed your website needs. Request a free quote NOW to know the exact cost.

  • How much time does it take to build and launch a website?

    The time it takes to build and launch a website also depends on your website needs. The more the requirements, the more complex the website and vice versa. Complex websites take more time to build than simple single-purpose websites. The time ranges from several weeks to a few months.

  • Do you provide website maintenance services?

    Yes, I provide website maintenance services. If you need these services, feel free to contact me now.

  • What platforms and technologies do you use for website development?

    To reduce the design time, I start with BootStrap 5 templates or WordPress themes which I customize to suit your website needs as discussed. For more complex websites that require a CMS, I use WordPress. I also use Elementor and Webflow website builders in special cases.

  • Can I update the website content myself?

    Yes, If that is what you prefer. I can create a website that requires no technical skills to update the content. Otherwise, I will be your web designer to help you update the content and save you the time to work on more important tasks.

  • Do you offer responsive design for mobile devices?

    Yes, I understand that 58% of all website traffic comes from mobile devices. I always ensure my website solutions are responsive on all devices, render perfectly and provide excellent user experience.

  • Will the website be optimized for search engines?

    Yes, all the websites I design are search engine optimized. Your customers and prospects can find you through a Google search.

  • How do you handle website hosting and domain registration?

    The domain registration and website hosting payment process is usually flexible. You are free to do it yourself if that is what you prefer. But if you need my help, I am always glad to help.

  • What security measures do you implement for websites?

    To ensure my clients' websites are secure, here are some of the security measures I implement
    Creating complex website passwords that I update regularly in website maintenance services
    Adding an SSL certificate and HTTPS to secure data transfer and personal information
    Choosing a Content Management System that help defend the website and with minimal security gaps
    Ensure the plugins and add-ons are up to date on CMS websites
    Ensure I work with hosting service providers that offer daily website files backup
    Continually test clients’ websites on maintenance plan to identify website areas that need improvement and added security measures.

  • What happens if there are issues with the website after it's launched?

    If your website has design issues after launching, I can always help you fix them.

  • What is your design process like?

    My design process is made up of four main stages
    1. Client interview: Here we discuss your goals, challenges, and the solution a new website presents
    2. Planning: Here I can create a website plan and website content outline.
    3. Building: Here I create the website content and build the website
    4. Implementation: Here I launch the fully functional website and request your feedback

  • Can you help with branding and logo design?

    No, I do not offer branding and logo design services - it’s not my strong area. I only design websites and marketing posters.

  • Do you provide content creation services?

    If you need a website and have no budget to hire a copywriter, I can help you craft the website copy for the website. I do not offer any other form of content creation services, only web copywriting.

  • Can you implement specific features or functionalities on my website?

    Yeah, I can once we discuss the specific features or functionalities you need on your website.

  • What is your payment structure?

    For a website design project and poster design, I use a fixed payment structure - I charge per project. For website maintenance services I use a fixed monthly payment structure while for small tasks I use an hourly payment structure.

  • What type of support do you offer after the website is live?

    Once the website is live, I can help you maintain the website to ensure it's secure, updated, has no issues, and much more at an affordable monthly fee.

  • What happens if I want to make changes to the website in the future?

    You are free to do so. If you would want my help in making the desired changes, I’ll be happy to help you. You are also free to work with another designer
